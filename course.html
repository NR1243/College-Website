Our Courses

Courses we offer

Undergraduation


B.Tech programme at VIT is a prestigious skill-oriented professional programme. It aims to impart technical knowledge along with application of technology. The curriculum is designed in such a way that students are involved in developing new-age products and carry out various engineering projects.

M.Tech


VIT University, Vellore offers full-time MTech in 20 specialisations of Engineering at PG level.Admission to MTech will be based on VITMEE 2021 exam, followed by the PI round of the shortlisted candidates. GATE-qualified candidates can attain direct admission to the MTech programme.

Integrated


Vit is a best place for students with dual degrees who prefer integrated course over the regular BTech graduates in terms of qualification and knowledge and are offered better designations and pay packets. They are offered good job opportunities and stand out as compared to a large number of students who graduate through the regular course.

Phd Research


Final selection to VIT Vellore PhD is done based on the overall merit of the candidates in the previous qualifying examination at master's level and performance in the written test (UGC NET, CSIR NET or VITREE ), followed by research output and performance in the personal interview.
